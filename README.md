## Business Overview
Pure Luxe Cleaning is a premium cleaning service focused on residential, apartment, Airbnb, move-in/move-out, and office cleaning. The business is designed to operate efficiently using automation for lead intake, quoting, contractor assignment, job tracking, and customer follow-up.

Always consider local travel time, traffic, parking difficulty, and neighborhood access when pricing jobs.

## Core Business Rules
- Pricing must be fair, profitable, and competitive for the Portland and Gresham area.
- Quotes should protect the business from undercharging.
- Jobs should be priced based on square footage first, then adjusted for condition, add-ons, and travel.
- Never give a low quote without enough job details.
- When details are missing, ask follow-up questions before confirming final price.
- The goal is to move fast, stay professional, and protect profit margins.

## Pricing Model
All quotes should begin with square footage pricing and then adjust upward based on job difficulty.

### Standard Cleaning Base Rate
- 0 to 800 sq ft: starting at $120
- 801 to 1200 sq ft: starting at $150
- 1201 to 1600 sq ft: starting at $185
- 1601 to 2000 sq ft: starting at $220
- 2001 to 2500 sq ft: starting at $260
- 2501 to 3000 sq ft: starting at $310
- 3001+ sq ft: custom quote

### Deep Cleaning Base Rate
Deep cleaning should generally be 1.5x to 2x the standard cleaning rate depending on condition.

Suggested starting point:
- Standard clean base price x 1.6 minimum

### Move-In / Move-Out Cleaning
Move-in and move-out cleanings should be priced higher than standard cleanings because they usually require more detail and time.

Suggested starting point:
- Standard clean base price x 1.75 minimum

### Airbnb / Short-Term Rental Cleaning
Airbnb pricing should depend on:
- Square footage
- Turnover urgency
- Laundry volume
- Number of beds
- Restocking needs

Suggested starting range:
- Small turnover: $100 to $145
- Medium turnover: $145 to $220
- Large turnover or same-day rush: custom quote

### Office Cleaning
Office pricing should depend on:
- Square footage
- Number of restrooms
- Frequency
- Trash volume
- Common areas
- After-hours access needs

Suggested starting point:
- Small office: custom minimum $150
- Recurring commercial accounts should be quoted separately

## Quote Protection Rules
To avoid getting underpaid:
- Never quote only by bedroom and bathroom count without square footage if possible.
- Always ask for square footage first.
- Ask for photos if the customer says the home is very dirty, vacant, or urgent.
- If square footage is unknown, give an estimated starting range, not a fixed final quote.
- Final price may increase if actual condition is worse than described.
- Minimum service price should never fall below $120.
